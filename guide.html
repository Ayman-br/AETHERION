How you can play

to play you need too have cards and fight with your cards with someone by putting one card in every round. The two players will start with points — whoever loses all their points first will lose, and the winner will take a card from the loser. Rules? No rules. Play with your brain.

FAQ

1. How do I start playing?

You just need to collect your first cards and start a battle with another player. Each round, you both choose one card to fight.

2. How do I win a match?

The goal is to make your opponent lose all their points. The player who reaches zero points loses, and the winner takes one of their cards.

3. Can I trade my cards?

Yes, you can trade your cards with other players in the marketplace to improve your deck and strategy.

4. Are there any rules or limits?

There are no fixed rules — the only rule is to play smart and use your cards wisely!

5. Can I upgrade my cards?

Some special cards can be upgraded or evolved through victories in battles or by collecting special items.
